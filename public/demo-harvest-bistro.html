Our Story

Farm-to-Table Since 2012

Harvest Bistro was born from a simple belief: that the finest dining begins with the finest ingredients. We partner with 18 local farms across Maharashtra to bring you produce at its seasonal peak.

Our head chef, Rohit Nair, trained under Michelin-starred kitchens in London and Lyon before returning to Mumbai to champion modern Indian fine dining.
